Tanmay Singh

biography

Scout, aka Tanmay Singh, is a 27-year-old Indian gaming YouTuber and esports athlete known for his laser-sharp accuracy and aggressive entry fragging in BGMI Esports. He currently streams and competes with TeamXSpark, showcasing his skills in various games alongside vlogs and mobile reviews for his 4.91 million subscribers. From Fnatic to Orange Rock and now TeamXSpark, Scout's career boasts impressive wins and continues to inspire with his motto: ''Never Give Up.'

Tanmay Singh was born July 30, 1996. He is known for his aggressive gameplay.
